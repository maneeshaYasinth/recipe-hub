Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Toast baguette slices until golden brown
  2. Mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper
  3. Spoon mixture onto toasted bread slices
  4. Serve immediately

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 20 large mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Remove mushroom stems and chop finely
  3. Mix stems with cream cheese, Parmesan, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper
  4. Stuff mushroom caps with mixture
  5. Bake for 20 minutes until golden
